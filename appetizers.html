  • Parsley

2. Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes
  • Fresh basil
  • Olive oil
  • Toasted bread

Steps:

  1. Chop tomatoes and basil.
  2. Mix with olive oil and salt.
  3. Spoon mixture onto toasted bread.
  4. Serve immediately.
