2. Fruit Salad

Ingredients:

  • Strawberries
  • Bananas
  • Blueberries
  • Honey
  • Lime juice

Steps:

  1. Wash and chop all fruits.
  2. Place in a large bowl.
  3. Drizzle with honey and lime juice.
  4. Toss gently to coat.
